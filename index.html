Proactive AI for Intelligent Traffic Control

ProLight develops next-generation traffic control systems that anticipate disruptions, predict network dynamics, and optimize urban mobility through simulation, graph neural networks, model predictive control, and search-based planning.

Vision

From reactive traffic control to proactive network intelligence.

Urban traffic networks are complex, uncertain, and vulnerable to disruptions. ProLight investigates how AI systems can predict future traffic states, reason over network-level interactions, and make robust control decisions before congestion propagates.

Research Systems

Core components of ProLight

T-REX

A benchmark environment for evaluating traffic signal control algorithms under incidents, uncertainty, and network-level disruptions.

GNN-MPC

A graph neural network model predictive control framework for proactive traffic signal optimization across urban networks.

GNN-AlphaZero

A search-based planning approach combining graph learning, simulation, and AlphaZero-style decision-making for traffic control.
