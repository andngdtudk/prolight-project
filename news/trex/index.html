Getting Started with T-REX

ProLight Project · 2026

T-REX is a simulation environment for training and evaluating AI-based traffic signal control algorithms under incidents, uncertainty, and network-level disruptions.

Why T-REX?

Traditional traffic signal control benchmarks often evaluate algorithms under recurrent demand patterns. T-REX focuses on incident-aware evaluation, where disruptions can propagate across the network and challenge the robustness of control policies.
