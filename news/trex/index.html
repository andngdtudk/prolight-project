Overview

T-REX is a simulation environment for training and evaluating AI-based traffic signal control algorithms under incidents and network-level disruptions.

Why T-REX?

Most traffic signal control benchmarks focus on recurrent congestion. T-REX focuses on robustness under non-recurrent disruptions such as lane blockages, incidents, and uncertain driver responses.
